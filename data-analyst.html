Data Analyst

Role Scope

London | Hybrid | Full-time | Required Experience: 2-3 years

Support enterprise finance and leasing teams with data validation, reporting, and release analytics. You will work closely with analysts and engineers to translate data into actionable insights, improve data quality, and maintain reporting accuracy during platform changes.

Main Responsibilities

  • Build and maintain dashboards and operational reports for finance and leasing workflows.
  • Validate data quality using SQL checks, reconciliation logic, and exception analysis.
  • Support UAT cycles by preparing test data and analyzing post-release performance metrics.
  • Partner with system analysts to map data definitions and reporting requirements.
  • Investigate data issues and propose fixes with clear documentation.

Required Profile

  • 2-3 years of experience in data analysis, reporting, or analytics roles.
  • Solid SQL skills with experience building queries for business reporting.
  • Experience with Excel, BI tools, or dashboarding platforms.
  • Ability to communicate data findings to business stakeholders.
