Lead Systems Analyst

Role Scope

London | Hybrid | Full-time | Required Experience: 8+ years

This is a delivery-critical analyst leadership role for enterprise finance and leasing products. You will own requirements fidelity, implementation clarity, UAT confidence, and release governance across multi-team environments.

Main Responsibilities

  • Lead requirement workshops and convert business needs into RS/FS and sprint-ready artifacts.
  • Drive traceability between user stories, test outcomes, and release decisions.
  • Conduct SQL-level validation to support behavior analysis and issue diagnostics.
  • Coordinate architecture, QA, and engineering teams during UAT and cutover phases.
  • Control post-release triage and stabilization with SLA-focused communication.
  • Mentor junior analysts and enforce documentation and operating standards.

Required Profile

  • Strong enterprise background in finance, leasing, ERP, or contract lifecycle systems.
  • Advanced SQL competence with data integrity and performance awareness.
  • Practical experience with API integrations and ETL validation workflows.
  • Confident stakeholder communication in high-pressure production contexts.
