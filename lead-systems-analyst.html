Lead Systems Analyst (Finance & Leasing Platforms)

London | Hybrid | Full-time | Required Experience: 8+ years

You will lead requirements-to-release delivery for leasing and finance platforms used by enterprise clients. This role combines business analysis, SQL/data diagnostics, API integration planning, and production-critical stakeholder coordination.

Main Responsibilities

  • Lead workshops and convert business needs into RS, FS, stories, and technical handover documents.
  • Act as bridge between product owners, architects, QA, and engineering teams.
  • Validate system behavior through data analysis, SQL diagnostics, and integration traceability.
  • Own UAT readiness, cutover planning, go-live checkpoints, and post-release issue governance.
  • Coach junior analysts and enforce documentation and delivery standards.

Required Profile

  • Strong enterprise background in finance, leasing, ERP, or contract management systems.
  • Advanced SQL/T-SQL/PL-SQL with performance and data-model awareness.
  • Hands-on experience with APIs, ETL/data pipelines, and production support workflows.
  • Proven stakeholder communication and incident/priority management under SLA.
